<h2>Early Literacy</h2>
<p>
Children who are read to from birth have a larger vocabulary and have a higher success rate of being lifelong readers and learners.&nbsp
<strong><u>Reading to our children will not only instill a love of reading but will expand their vocabulary and build comprehension skills.</strong></u>

<h5>About Assistive Technology:</h5>
<p>
Most branches have an assistive technology workstation designed for use by people who use a wheelchair, have low vision, are blind, or have a reading disability.<br> This station has a height-adjustable table, a magnifier that reads aloud printed text, and a computer with assistive software. <br>If you would like to use this workstation, please choose the <strong><u> "ADA" or "ATW" queue</strong></u> when signing up to use a public computer.
